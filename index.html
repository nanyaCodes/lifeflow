For Hospitals

Need Blood? We’ve Got You Covered

LifeFlow connects your hospital to a growing network of voluntary blood donors. Whether you're managing a blood bank or facing an urgent shortage, our platform helps you find compatible donors quickly and efficiently. Sign up to register your hospital, post blood requests, and get matched with ready donors based on location, blood type, and availability. It’s fast, secure, and built with your patients in mind.
